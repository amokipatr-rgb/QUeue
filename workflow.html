SMQSS Workflow & System Guide

How the Smart Queue Management System works for officers, administrators, and the entire university community.

For Officers

1
Sign In

Log in with your officer number and PIN. Your desk appears on the public display automatically.

2
Call Next

One click calls the next waiting client to your window. No shouting, no confusion.

3
Serve & Complete

Mark the transaction done. The queue moves forward and data is logged for reporting.

For Administrators

Manage Offices

Add, edit, and reorganise service offices. Assign officers, set availability status, and control service offerings per office.

Monitor Performance

Live queue statistics, wait times, service volumes, and completed transactions across every office — all in one dashboard.

System Configuration

Manage services, token counters, PIN resets, and system-wide settings. Full audit trail of all administrative actions.

Review Feedback

After each service, ratings from clients help track and improve desk performance. Monitor satisfaction trends across all offices.

Attendance & Analytics

Track officer weekly attendance with login times, device info, and IP addresses. Analyse availability patterns, daily hours logged, and tokens served per officer — all in a single weekly report with CSV export.

Benefits

Eliminate Paper Queues

Fully digital queue management — no more manual registers, lost names, or crowded counters.

Real-Time Visibility

Know exactly what is happening at every desk across campus — waiting counts, service times, and officer availability.

Fair & Orderly Service

First-come, first-served with priority assistance for persons with disabilities. No queue jumping, no favouritism.

Data-Driven Decisions

Reports on wait times, service volumes, student feedback, and peak hours help leadership optimise resource allocation.
